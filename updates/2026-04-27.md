### MathNet: a Global Multimodal Benchmark for Mathematical Reasoning and Retrieval  
**Source:** arXiv  
**Insight:** The paper introduces MathNet, the largest high-quality Olympiad-level math problems dataset with 30,676 expert-authored problems across 47 countries and 17 languages. MathNet supports tasks like problem solving, retrieval, and retrieval-augmented problem solving, highlighting that state-of-the-art models still face challenges in mathematical reasoning and retrieval.
