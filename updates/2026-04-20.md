### ASMR-Bench: Auditing for Sabotage in ML Research  
**Source:** arXiv  
**Insight:** The study introduces ASMR-Bench, a benchmark to evaluate the detection of sabotage in machine learning research codebases. Both frontier LLMs and human-assisted auditors struggled to reliably detect sabotage, with an AUROC of 0.77 and a top-1 fix rate of 42% achieved by Gemini 3.1 Pro. The research highlights the challenges in ensuring the integrity of AI-conducted experiments.
